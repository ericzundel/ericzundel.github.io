Lasagna

A lovely dish you can make in just 4 hours!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Italian Sausage
  • Mushrooms
  • Your favorite ingredients for a red sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Shredded Mozarella
  • Shredded Parmasean Cheese

Preparation

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the italian sausage
  3. Cook some mushrooms
  4. Cook a sauce
  5. Add the sausage to the sauce
  6. Make the filling with Ricotta Cheese
  7. Make layers as follows:
    1. Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta cheese filling
    4. shredded cheeses
  8. Repeat layers until the dish is full
  9. Bake for an hour or so
  10. Forget to take it out of the oven
  11. Enjoy!
