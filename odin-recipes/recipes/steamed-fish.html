Steamed Fish

A great way to cook a delicate white fish like tilapia, crappie, or bass taught to me by Fernando.

Ingredients

  • Skillet with a lid
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • slices of onion, medium thickness
  • thin fish fillets
  • slices of lemon (optional)
  • other spices (optional)

Preparation

  1. Pat the fish dry and add salt
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet to medium heat
  3. Brown the fish on one side for one minute
  4. Flip the fish and brown for one more minute
  5. Remove the fish and add the onion. Brown it if you like
  6. Place the fish on top of the onion.
  7. Optionally add your other spices
  8. Put the lid on and turn heat to low. You can even turn it off completely.
  9. Leave the skillet on the stove for 10 minutes
  10. Optionally squeeze lemon on the fish
